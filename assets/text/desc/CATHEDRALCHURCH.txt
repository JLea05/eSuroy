DESCRIPTION:   The Sr. San Nicolas De Tolentino City Cathedral of Surigao del Norte is a majestic religious landmark located in the heart of Surigao City, Philippines. As the primary seat of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Surigao, this cathedral holds great significance for the local community and draws visitors from all walks of life.  Cathedral's architecture blends modern and traditional elements, showcasing complex surface and elegant designs, giving spiritual grandeur and an iconic silhouette.

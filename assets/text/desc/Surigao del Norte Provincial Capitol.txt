CAPITOL
DESCRIPTION:    The Surigao del Norte Provincial Capitol is a stunning example of modern architecture that incorporates aspects of the local culture. The capital, which is situated in the province's center and has numerous government agencies and offices, acts as the area's administrative and political hub. Inside, there are educational displays showcasing the history and natural beauties of the area. The Provincial Capitol, a representation of development and harmony, is a source of pride for Surigao del Norte and a sign of the province's promising future.

BOULEVARD
DESCRIPTION: Surigao del Norte's City Boulevard will offer a mesmerizing coastal experience, allowing guests to stroll along the beautiful shoreline and take in breath-taking Pacific Ocean views. The boulevard has been decorated with colorful gardens and artwork, which creates a gorgeous atmosphere for leisure time. The street is lined with small shops and food stands that serve a variety of mouthwatering seafood dishes and traditional Filipino cuisine. City Boulevard continues to be a must-see location for tourists looking for a memorable coastal retreat in Surigao del Norte because to its contemporary amenities and warm ambiance.
